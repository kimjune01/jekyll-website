Our Process

We start with a conversation about your goals, then develop a plan with you.
Depending on the project's current progress, this is how we break it down:

  • Discovery — define possibilities and goals for the project
  • UX Prototype — solidify the experience to build with your team and customers
  • Build — iterate upon the prototype for feedback from your potential customers, then improve
  • Deployment — operate and maintain the application for years to come; we can even help train your team
